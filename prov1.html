“Instagoraphobia”
  - A fear of crowded
    internet places





Instant Digital Empathy
 rapidly alters our
 moods and emotions.

Feeling anything,
 and many things,
 from a distance
 will continuously
 wreak havoc on our society





Thoughts are things,
 they have mass.

Compounding mass increases
 gravitational pull.

Increased gravity
 will speed up
 the flow of Time.





As we create more space
 to allocate information,
 we dig a hole
 deeper and deeper
 into a universe
 of our own design






We’re all just
 bites and bytes
 of our neighbors memory






Questions of the unknown
 become self evident
 with patience






What must I do
 to once more touch infinity?






Do black holes
 eat passing light
 and produce gravity?






Do oscillations in gravity
 create a frequency
 to move Time?






Time moves slow &
 Life climbs through it.

Bound by gravity,
 separated by death.






How do I describe zero
 when anything I say
 describes what it is not?






What is my reality?

How can I share it
 in enough detail
 for someone else
 to understand completely?





Cradle me,
 I am infant
 once again.

I’ve forgotten all
 I thought I’ve known.
